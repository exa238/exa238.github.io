Mac and Cheeze Stuffed Baked Potatoes with Crispy Mushrooms

Ingredients

For the Mac and Cheeze

  • 1 box of protein pasta*
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 medium-sized carrots
  • 1 large potato
  • 2 tablespoons of miso paste
  • 2 +1/2 cup of reduced-sodium vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of roasted garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 2 heaping tablespoons of nutritional yeast
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/3 cup of raw, unsalted cashews

For the Crispy Mushrooms

  • 2 large portobello mushroom caps
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of roasted garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon of liquid smoke
  • 1/2 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of a neutral flavored oil*
  • 1/4 teaspoon of onion salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin

Remaining Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon of vegan butter*
  • 2 tablespoons of a topping of your choice*

Instructions

  1. Prehead oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Fill a medium pot halfway with water and salt and bring to a boil. Once boiling, add in your pasta and cook to the box's instructions. Drain pasta and set aside.
  3. Rinse and roughly chop 1 large potato, 2 medium-sized carrots, 2 portobello mushrooms, and 1 small onion into pieces. Add the carrots, onion, and potato to a medium sized pot along with the cashews and 2 cups of vegetable broth. Bring to a boil.
  4. Place your portobello mushroom pieces in a small bowl and add in oil, liquid smoke, and seasonings. Add the mushrooms to a parchment-covered pan and roast in the oven along with the remaining two potatoes.
  5. Once the boiling veggies are fork-tender, drain the water and add to a high speed blender. Blend the ingredients along with miso paste, the remaining veggie broth, nutritinal yeast, and spices until smooth.
  6. Add the sauce to the protein pasta and mix until thoroughly combined.
  7. Remove the mushrooms and potatoes from the oven. Once cooled slightly, cut open each potato and add vegan butter (if desired), mac and cheeze, and top with crispy mushrooms and any other toppings of choice.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy.

Notes

  • *I used protein pasta (Banza) for it's extra protein but feel free to use any other gluten free or wheat pasta alternative.
  • *I use extra virgin olive oil but feel free to use any oil of your choosing or none at all. If you choose to use none, replace with a 2 tablespoons of water.
  • *I use vegan butter to moisten the potato but feel free to omit if desired.
  • I enjoy chives, vegan parmesan, and red pepper flakes, but feel free to use any topping of choice.
