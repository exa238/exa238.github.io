Tofu Scramble

Ingredients

  • 16 oz of high protein, extra firm tofu*
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt*
  • 1/2 teaspoon of 'Trader Joes Mushroom & Co. Multipurpose Umami Seasoning Blend'
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon of dill
  • 1/2 teaspoon of roasted garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin
  • 2 heaping tablespoons of nutritional yeast*
  • 4 medium-sized cloves of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of a neutral flavored oil*

Instructions

  1. Chop your cloves of garlic and set aside.
  2. Remove your tofu from its package, drain the water, and dry it with a kitchen towel. Then, crumble it into small pieces (the texture should resemble a scramble).
  3. Heat up a skillet to medium heat and add in your oil.
  4. Add in your chopped garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  5. Next add your tofu crumbles along with the all of the seasonings.
  6. Stir over medium heat for 5-7 minutes and until the seasonings are well-incorporated and the flavors are evenly distributed.
  7. Serve immediately. (I like to serve mine on avocado toast but feel free to enjoy with breakfast potatoes, pancakes, or any side of your choosing)

Notes

  • *Extra-firm tofu is recommended for it's crumbly factor. Softer tofus (like silken) will not crumble and give you the crumbly texture you're looking for.
  • *Feel free to use any salt here, however, Kala Namak would add the sulfur-type flavor synonymous with eggs.
  • *You can use a plain, fortified nutritional yeast or opt for a flavored version as well.
  • *I used extra virgin olive oil but feel free to use any oil of your choice (like safflower or canola).
